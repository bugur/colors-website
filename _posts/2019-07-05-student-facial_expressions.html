Completed By: Bilgehan Nal
Term Completed:: Summer 2017 as Summer Internship

Brief Information The aim of the project is possess Baxter with a face including mouth, eye and eyebrows for different facial expressions. In addition enabling it with the ability to track its own end effector with its eyes and hand, or with help of its sonar sensors an object around it. Then control this face using an application for pc or Android phone.
